When should I do this?

Confidential reports are restricted for privacy and security reasons. They are not shown in full in Active Reports or the weekly board digest.

Directors may see that a confidential report has been lodged, including the date, issue type, general category, and status. The full report is accessible only to the GM, Club Secretary, and Board Chair. Directors who wish to access for a relevant governance purpose may request it through the Chair or Secretary.
